Nines, A Virtual Closet App: Case Study


NINES is a mobile app that allows users to keep inventory of their wardrobes and track and plan outfits to wear.

This app was developed and pitched as an alternative to other virtual closet apps that target specific genders, have exhausting user experience, and lack cloud back-up.

The Challenge

Develop user personas for NINES, a mobile app that allows users to keep inventory of their wardrobes, track, and plan outfits to wear. This app should appeal to both men and women who are very tech-savvy.

To develop these personas, research was gathered on user reviews and demographic breakdowns from current virtual closet apps, fashion forum posts, and posts from various fashion bloggers.

Three personas were developed from this research.

From these personas, certain functions of the app were proposed to provide value over other current apps in the market.
